# Acme Corp - Customer Support FAQ

## Orders & Shipping

**How long does shipping take?**
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. International orders take 10-15 business days.

**How do I track my order?**
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your order at acmecorp.com/track or directly on the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, or USPS depending on your region).

**Can I change or cancel my order?**
Orders can be changed or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, they enter our fulfillment system and cannot be modified. Contact support immediately if you need to cancel.

## Returns & Refunds

**What is your return policy?**
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and include the receipt. Sale items and digital products are final sale.

**How do I start a return?**
1. Go to acmecorp.com/returns
2. Enter your order number and email
3. Select the items you want to return
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Drop off at any UPS location

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the return.

**Are returns free?**
Yes, we provide a prepaid return label for all returns within the US. International returns require the customer to pay return shipping.

## Products & Pricing

**Do you offer discounts?**
We offer a 10% discount for first-time customers using code WELCOME10. We also have seasonal sales and a loyalty program for repeat customers. Sign up for our newsletter to get notified.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We do not accept cryptocurrency.

**Do you offer a warranty?**
All products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranties (2 or 3 years) are available for purchase at checkout.

## Account & Technical

**How do I reset my password?**
Go to acmecorp.com/login and click "Forgot password." Enter your email address and we'll send a reset link within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

**How do I update my billing or shipping address?**
Log in to your account at acmecorp.com, go to Account Settings > Addresses to add or edit addresses. Note: you cannot change the address on an order that has already shipped.

**Is my payment information secure?**
Yes. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full credit card details. Payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified provider.

Our support team is available Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm EST. We typically respond to emails within 4 business hours. Live chat is available during support hours.
